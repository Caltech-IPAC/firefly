place holder only: The Details Pane: Getting More Information on a Row In Your Search Results from the NASA WISE Image Server

After you have searched the archive and explored the tabs in your search results, you will be interested in understanding the additional information that appears on the right side of your screen when a row in the table (for any tab) is highlighted. You can click and drag to resize window panes or column edges.

The contents of the Details pane change according to which tab you have in the foreground.

Please see Explanatory Supplement to the WISE Preliminary Data Release Products to better understand WISE data.
