Level 1b -- Single Frames

xxx Replace with PTF Level 1b refers to WISE individual frames, which are the single 8.8 second exposures from which the WISE survey was constructed. Columns for Level 1b searches are:
band - WISE band 1 (3.3 microns), 2 (4.5 microns), 3 (12 microns), 4 (22 microns)
magzp - magnitude of zero flux density in the image
magzpunc - uncertainty of magzp
scanid - identification of pole-to-pole orbit scan for observation
frameid - identification of frame number within a given scan
dateobs - UT observation date in standard format date-hms
mjd_obs - julian date of observation midpoint
dtanneal - seconds since anneal; anneal cycle is 12 hours
utanneal - UT date of last anneal
exptime - exposure time in second of frame
debgain - electronic gain of DEB; changes post-cryo
febgain - electronic gain of FEB; changes post-cryo
qa_status - preliminary versus final; currently all prelim
qual_frame - quality assessment of frame; 10 highest
qual_scan - quality assessment of scan; 5 is typical, 10 highest
date_imgprep - date of last processing
